Packrafting Eureka Creek

July 03, 2013 alaska, travel, photos

Over the 4th of July, my wife, my father-in-law and I decided to take a packrafting trip in Alaska. We did some research and found a cool trip that is described very well here: McClaren Summit to Eureka Creek Traverse by Ed Plumb of Fairbanks, AK. It rained a little bit, but other than that, the trip was great. I can't wait to do some more packrafting in the near future.

Oh, and we had to stop in Alaska Mountaineering & Hiking while we were in Anchorage!
